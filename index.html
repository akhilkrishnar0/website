ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Akhil, a Research Assistant at the Centre for Excellence in Astronomy and Astrophysics, Department of Physics, Christ University, Bangalore, India. 

I am passionate about unravelling the mysteries of galaxy formation and evolution, combining deep observations with innovative theoretical models. With expertise in both ground- and space-based observational astronomy, I explore the interplay between young and old stellar populations to uncover the dynamic processes shaping galaxies. I actively integrate data science techniques into astrophysics research, leveraging advanced methodologies to analyze complex datasets and drive discoveries.
